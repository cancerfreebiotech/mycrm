## April Highlights

**1｜AACR Annual Meeting 2026（4/17-22｜ San Diego） **

**The AACR Annual Meeting 2026** is one of the most prestigious cancer research conferences in the world, bringing together leading experts from academia, clinical practice, and industry to discuss the latest research findings and treatment trends.

Our team will be attending in person and presenting research posters, engaging in academic exchange with global research teams, furthering international scientific validation of our technologies in the **organoid** and **liquid biopsy** fields.

✦ Presentation Highlights ✦

- ***Extracellular viscosity modulates spheroid formation in SMARCB1-deficient brain tumor cells*** | 4/19 14:00-17:00, Poster Section 26, Poster Board Number 21

- ***Circulating tumor cell based ex vivo platform for characterizing circulating hybrid cell dynamics in multiple cancer types*** | 4/20 14:00-17:00, Poster Section 42, Poster Board Number 22

📅 Date: 2026/4/17 - 22
📍 Location: San Diego Convention Center

🔗｜ [Official Website](https://www.aacr.org/meeting/aacr-annual-meeting-2026/)

**2｜ CPHI JAPAN 2026（4/21-23｜Tokyo）**

**CPHI Japan 2026** is one of Asia's most important international pharmaceutical industry exhibitions, focused on drug development, manufacturing, and supply chain integration.

With the advancement of the **FDA Modernization Act 3.0**, **organoids** have been officially recognized as a viable alternative to animal testing, bringing a transformative new trend to the drug development process and opening up an expanded market opportunity for **EVA Select**.

We will be exhibiting at this event, and our **CEO Po** will be present to deliver a tech talk on **4/21 from 14:00–14:15**, engaging with global pharmaceutical companies and industry partners to explore application scenarios for **EVA Select** in drug development and cultivating collaboration opportunities with pharma companies and CROs.

📅 Date: 2026/4/21 - 23 10:00 – 18:00
📍 Location: Tokyo Big Sight East Hall3 3H-32 Taiwan Pharma Pavilion
(3-10-1 Ariake, Koto-ku, Tokyo)

🔗｜ [Official Website](https://www.cphi.com/japan/)

**3｜ SusHi Tech Tokyo 2026（4/27-29｜Tokyo） **

**SusHi Tech Tokyo 2026** is one of Japan's most prominent international innovation and technology events, focusing on sustainable urban development and forward-looking technology applications.

This year, we will be exhibiting at the **Taiwan Pavilion**, showcasing an **immersive VR experience** that allows visitors to personally experience the full process of avatar medicine, while also introducing the interface and real-world applications of **Prometheus Lab AI** — presenting our latest achievements in AI-driven healthcare.

We also co-hosted a **Startup Happy Hour** event the evening before the conference with **Meet Startups** and **CDIB Capital Innovation Advisors**, giving Taiwan and Japan startups, investors, and ecosystem partners an opportunity for more informal networking and exchange.

We look forward to expanding connections with international startups, enterprises, and investors, strengthening our presence in Asia's innovation ecosystem, and exploring new possibilities for cross-sector collaboration.

## March Recap

**1｜2026 Spring Taiwan Demo Day （3/14｜California）**

We participated in the **2026 Spring Taiwan Demo Day**, co-organized by **Taiwan Next Foundation**, **Startup Island TAIWAN**, **StarFab** and **TAI1 AI Accelerator** !

The event brought together AI startups, investors, and industry ecosystem partners from Taiwan and around the world to showcase the latest trends in AI applications. We took the stage to share how we leverage AI to advance our **EVA Select** and **Prometheus Lab AI** technologies.

Through interactions with international startups and investors, we received interest and inquiries from multiple investors, opened several potential collaboration opportunities, and further raised our profile within the international AI and precision medicine startup ecosystem, laying the groundwork for future global expansion.

**2｜ Taipei Smart City Summit & Expo（3/17 - 20｜Taipei） **

We showcased **Prometheus Lab AI** in the **Qualcomm exhibition area** at the **Taipei Smart City Summit & Expo**, introducing thousands of on-site visitors to how we are integrating AI into biotech and healthcare to enhance the efficiency and quality management of laboratory workflows.

The event brought together government agencies, enterprises, startups, and major tech companies, all demonstrating the latest AI applications across industries. We actively built connections, exchanged ideas on technology, and explored collaboration opportunities and new application domains.

**3｜G-1 Startup Grand Prix （3/20｜Taipei） **

Last month, we co-hosted the **G-1 Startup Grand Prix startup pitch competition** together with **Milk. Co., Ltd.**, and it was a great success!

The event brought together outstanding students from both Taiwan and Japan, while also connecting startup resources from both sides — including **Startup Island TAIWAN**, **Taipei Computer Association**, **CDIB Innovation Accelerator**, **Fukuoka Growth Next**, **IAPS**, and **i2i Accelerator** — further building cross-border ties between Taipei and Japan and laying the foundation for a long-term talent and innovation ecosystem.

Nearly 100 startup partners and investors attended on the day, witnessing the real-world achievements of young teams. Competing teams focused on diverse fields including AI, healthcare, and talent matching, demonstrating the next generation's innovative capacity to address social challenges.

Through this event, we not only deepened Taiwan-Japan bilateral innovation collaboration, but also progressively built a cross-border platform for talent and startup exchange, strengthening our long-term positioning in the Asia-Pacific innovation ecosystem.
