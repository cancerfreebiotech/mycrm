**Smart Lab: AI-Powered Precision Medicine **

For decades, advanced biotechnology laboratories have faced a dual challenge: **heavy reliance on individual expertise** and **limited standardization**. Addressing this challenge by integrating automation and AI into laboratory workflows has been our core mission. This vision led to the development of **Smart Lab**, our AI-powered intelligent laboratory platform.

**Smart Lab** deeply integrates **AI** into experimental design, image analysis, workflow documentation, and decision support, transforming every experiment and every data point into traceable, accumulative, and scalable assets.

Across more than 2,500 days of laboratory operations, we have continuously accumulated high-quality data and utilized **AI** to convert it into models that are replicable, verifiable, and scalable. This approach reduces failure rates and labor costs while elevating decision quality, making medical and biotech innovation more reliable and more responsive to real clinical needs.

This operating model has been validated on the global stage through **American Association of Cancer Research (AACR)** research publications, the **Qualcomm Innovate in Taiwan Challenge (QITC)** Finals, and **CES**, and is now being deployed across medical institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and academic research organizations.

Looking ahead, we will continue to build around **Smart Lab**, accelerating the global implementation of precision medicine.

## December Recap

**1｜Qualcomm Snapdragon Compute Ecosystem Summit 2025（12/2｜Taipei）**

Last month, our CEO Po was invited to speak at the **Snapdragon Compute Ecosystem Summit 2025**, hosted by **Qualcomm**. He introduced our latest product, **Prometheus Lab AI**, shared real-world application results, and discussed our future roadmap with global ecosystem partners and industry experts.

**Prometheus Lab AI** enables previously non-standardizable organoid workflows to become scalable and traceable. It significantly reduces labor and documentation costs while establishing reproducible operational standards for organoid technologies. Beyond improving laboratory management, this solution has become a key foundation for our international expansion and the commercialization of **Organoid Intelligence**.

**2｜AI Glasses Dev Day 2025（12/23｜Taipei）**

At the end of last month, we were invited by **HTC** to participate in the **AI Glasses Dev Day 2025**.

In addition to discussions on the future applications of smart glasses across industries, our Chief Strategy Officer, Kevin, took the stage to introduce and demonstrate **Prometheus Lab AI**. Through live demonstrations, nearly 100 attendees experienced how AI optimizes laboratory workflows and management.

This hands-on engagement increased product visibility and facilitated meaningful interactions with potential industry partners and customers—successfully expanding new business opportunities.

**3｜ 《The twilight hour》Patient Sharing: A Real Case of E.V.A. Select**

**《The twilight hour》** is a patient interview series we produce to document individual cancer journeys and share their stories of resilience with the public.

In this episode, we feature Little Lai, a young patient diagnosed with **neuroblastoma**. By the age of three and a half, he had already endured six rounds of chemotherapy, experienced recurrence, and undergone surgery—placing a significant burden on his body.

Toward the end of his second treatment phase, his family used our **E.V.A. Select** service to identify more suitable medication options and adjust his treatment pathway. As a result, his physical condition improved noticeably during therapy.

Through these interviews, we hope to offer strength and comfort to patients and families facing cancer, while increasing awareness of **E.V.A. Select**, providing patients with one more option and one more source of hope at critical moments.
