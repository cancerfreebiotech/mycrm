**5｜ 2026 WIC APAC（3/28-29｜Taipei） **

We will participate in the **World Immunotherapy Council Asia-Pacific Conference (WIC-APAC 2026)**, where we will present our **EVA Select** technology and **Prometheus Lab AI** to leading experts, clinicians, and researchers from Europe, the United States, and the Asia-Pacific region.

With the rapid development of immunotherapy, accurately evaluating patient responses to treatment has become a critical challenge in precision oncology.

Our **EVA Select** technology helps reduce the likelihood of ineffective treatments, while **Prometheus Lab AI** improves efficiency and traceability in immunotherapy-related research.

Through this conference, we hope to engage with global experts to explore the potential applications of these technologies in cancer immunotherapy, develop future collaborations, and further enhance our visibility within the global precision medicine and immunotherapy communities.
