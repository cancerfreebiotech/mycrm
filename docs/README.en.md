# myCRM Guide

myCRM is the company-wide customer relationship management system used across cancerfree.io. The core flow is to snap a business card with the Telegram Bot, let the AI recognize it automatically, and save it to the CRM with one tap; from there you manage contacts, notes, tasks, reports, and newsletters together in the web interface.

> Current version: **v7.7.0**
