# Contact Management

Path: `/contacts`

---

## Contact List

### Search & Filter

- **Keyword search**: Matches name, Email, company name (fuzzy search)
- **Tag filter**: Click a Tag button to show only contacts with that Tag
- **Sort**: By creation time descending (newest first)
- **Pagination**: 20 per page, with page navigation at the bottom

### Export

Click "Export Excel" or "Export CSV" to download the current filtered results (not limited by pagination).

---

## Add Contact

Path: `/contacts/new`

Divided into four sections for manual entry:

| Section | Fields |
|---------|--------|
| **Basic Info** | Name (Chinese/English/Local), Company (Chinese/English/Local), Title |
| **Contact Info** | Email, Second Email, Phone, Second Phone, Address, Website, **Country** |
| **Social Media** | LinkedIn, Facebook |
| **Notes** | Notes text |

### Business Card AI Recognition (Multiple Cards)

1. Click "Select Business Card Photos" — select up to **6 photos** at once (front, back, etc.)
2. Click "Scan Business Cards" — the system compresses and merges them for AI recognition
3. The left side of the page shows photo previews; the right side shows the AI recognition results side-by-side
4. Click "Apply to Form" to fill in fields, or "Ignore" to fill in manually

> Before saving, the system checks for duplicate contacts by comparing Email and name similarity, and prompts for confirmation if a duplicate is detected.

---

## Batch Upload

Path: `/contacts/batch-upload`

Upload up to **50** business card photos at once:

1. Click or drag to upload images (JPG / PNG supported)
2. The system processes up to 5 at a time (parallel upload + AI recognition)
3. A progress bar shows current progress
4. After recognition completes, a preview table is shown — each entry can be edited individually
5. Batch duplicate detection (compared against existing contacts)
6. Click "Save All" to save at once

## Contact Details

Path: `/contacts/[id]`

### Basic Info Section

Displays all contact fields; click "Edit" in the top-right corner to open the Edit Modal.

- Copy icon next to **Email / Phone** fields — click to copy to clipboard
- **Country** field displays flag emoji + name (e.g. 🇹🇼 Taiwan)

### Business Card Management

A single contact can have multiple business cards (front, back, or custom labels):
- Select up to **6 photos** at once; AI merges them for recognition and shows differing fields for confirmation
- Click 🗑 in the top-right corner of a card to delete it

### Interaction Log Timeline

Displays all records in reverse chronological order. Types include:
- **Note** (note)
- **Meeting** (meeting)
- **Email** (email)
- **System** (system) — auto-generated by the system

Loads 20 entries by default; scrolling to the bottom automatically loads more (infinite scroll).

### Send Email

Click the "Send Email" button to open the enhanced email Modal:
- **To**: Editable, defaults to the contact's Email
- **Template**: Selecting one auto-fills the subject, body, and attachments
- **AI Generation**: Enter a description; AI uses the recipient's info and recent interaction records to generate the body
- **Temporary Attachments**: Upload attachments for this send only (max 2MB per file); not saved to the template

An Email interaction record is automatically created after sending.
